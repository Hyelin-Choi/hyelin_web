**Institution:** Korea Institute for Advanced Study (KIAS)  
**Period:** Summer 2024  

### Overview
Attended the [15th KIAS CAC Summer School on Artificial Intelligence & Parallel Computing](https://cac.kias.re.kr/2024/), which provided intensive training in parallel computing (MPI), scientific computing, physics-informed neural networks (PINN), Bayesian inference, and diffusion models.

### Contributions
- Completed coursework and hands-on training in MPI-based parallel computing and scientific computing  
- Developed and presented a project on solving the **Merton Portfolio Problem** using PINN  
- Awarded **3rd Place, Project Award** for the presentation
