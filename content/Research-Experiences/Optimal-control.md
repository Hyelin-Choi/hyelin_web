**Institution:** Sungkyunkwan University  
**Advisor:** [Prof. Woocheol Choi](https://sites.google.com/site/wchoiam)  
**Period:** Jan. 2022 – Dec. 2022  

### Overview
Engaged in undergraduate research on optimal control theory and its modern extensions, combining classical control methods with machine learning and data-driven approaches.

### Contributions
- Studied theoretical foundations of optimal control, including **Kalman filtering** and **model predictive control (MPC)**  
- Explored machine learning approaches such as **contrastive learning** for control tasks  
- Investigated data-driven dynamical systems frameworks, including **Koopman operator theory**, for applications in control
- Studied the **Ensemble-Based Goal-biased RRT (EBG-RRT)** algorithm for dynamic path planning and its connection with **Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)**
- Provided **tutoring sessions** on **GNNs** for both undergraduate and graduate students
