### Overview
This project investigated the use of **Regularized Direct Policy Optimization (RDPO)** as a reinforcement learning approach for solving linear quadratic control (LQR) problems.  
The performance of RDPO was evaluated and compared against classical optimal control solutions.

### Contributions
- Implemented the RDPO algorithm for LQR problems
